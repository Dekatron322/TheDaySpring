CEDAR COLLEGE ADMISSION

Admission form is to be obtained from the school, properly filled and submitted to academic office. The student must be at least 10 years old. An entrance examination that includes assessment in Mathematics, English Language and Social Studies will be taken. Examination centre is at the Cedar College, this will be communicated to you. Admission will be based on Merit.

ACADEMIC YEAR

The school academic year runs from September through July of the following year

CURRICULUM

The College runs the National Junior and Secondary programme which conforms to the National Policy of Education.

TUITION FEES

The fee charged by the college covers tuition, administrative and maintenance cost. All fees must be paid on or before the first day of every term. For further information and enquiry, contact the school or email the school at college@thedayspringschools.com.ng
